Volunteer Inquiry for Interaction Week 2017

If you’d like to learn more about volunteer opportunities at Interaction 17, please tell us more about how you’d like to be involved below!

Some important facts:

  • There are pre-event tasks and on-site tasks. On-site tasks require that you be available full-time from Feb. 4-8.
  • On-site Volunteer shifts will be scheduled from Sunday-Wednesday, 7:00am-12:00pm and 12:00pm-5:00pm. All Volunteers must be available for setup at 12pm on Saturday, February 4 and registration at 7am on Monday, Feb. 6.
  • Pre-event tasks will vary by committee and the time commitment will vary depending on the committee’s responsibilities.
    While you will receive a complimentary ticket to the conference, we expect our volunteers to primarily be available to assist conference staff, speakers, and venue personal as needed when needed.
  • Interaction17 is not able to provide a stipend for transportation or lodging.
